# Contributing to HYPERFOCUS-UNIFIED-EMPIRE

First off — thank you! 🧠 This is the master hub for the entire HyperFocus Zone ecosystem.

## 🌟 How to Contribute

1. **Fork** the repo
2. **Create a branch** — `git checkout -b feature/your-idea`
3. **Make your changes** — keep commits small and focused
4. **Push** — `git push origin feature/your-idea`
5. **Open a PR** — describe what you changed and why

## ⚠️ Rules

- Never commit `.env` files or secrets
- Always `git fetch` before pushing
- Check `WHATS_DONE.md` before building something that might exist

## 💬 Questions?

Open a [Discussion](https://github.com/welshDog/HYPERFOCUS-UNIFIED-EMPIRE/discussions) or check the ecosystem docs.
